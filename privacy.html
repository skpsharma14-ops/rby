Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Promise

Story Sprouts is made for fun! We don't ask for your name or create accounts. Your privacy is super important to us.

What Info We Use

  • Reading Progress: Your reading history is saved on your own computer or tablet. We can't see it!
  • Contact Form: If you or your parents send us a message, we'll use your email only to write back.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. You can read all our books without them.

Your Choices

  • You can ask a grown-up to help you clear your browser's data to erase your reading history and start fresh.
  • Using a private or incognito window in your browser means your reading history won't be saved.

Questions?

If you have any questions, please ask a parent to help you use the contact form on our home page.
