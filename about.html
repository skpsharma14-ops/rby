About Story Sprouts

Our Mission

Story Sprouts was created with a simple goal: to provide wonderful story books that are easy for kids to read right away. We believe fun stories should be free and safe for everyone, without needing any downloads or grown-up help.

We want every kid to be able to enjoy a good book, whether they're on a computer, a tablet, or a phone. Just click a button and start reading!
