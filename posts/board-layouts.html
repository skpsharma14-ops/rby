Top 5 Tips for Faster Racing!

Get our best secrets for winning and getting a new high score!

Published 2025-08-22

Want to get a new high score? Check out these five easy tips to help you zoom past the competition and become a racing champion.

1. Look Ahead

Don't just look at your car! Keep your eyes on the track ahead to see what's coming. This will give you more time to dodge obstacles.

2. Gentle Taps

You don't need to hold the buttons down. Use small, quick taps on the arrow keys to steer. This helps you make small changes and stay in control.

3. Find the Gaps

Look for the biggest open spaces between the trees. Aim for the middle of the gap to give yourself the most room.

4. Stay Calm

If you're about to crash, don't panic! Sometimes a quick move can save you. The more you play, the calmer you'll be.

5. Practice Makes Perfect!

The best way to get better is to keep playing. Every race helps you learn the game. Have fun and try to beat your own score!
